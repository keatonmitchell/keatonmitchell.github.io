Topographic Map Generator (procedural art)
A site that generates a topographic map.
Feel free to use any or all of the source code.
Inspired by The Wireframe God.

ZUGZWANG (1-act play)
A surreal comedy about a person who cannot win a game of chess.
Even as the show's narrator continually explains how to play like a master,
this unnamed novice gradually discovers that none of it helps whatsoever.
What do you do when there is nothing to do but fail? It's quite simple.

Gravity Platformer (minigame)
A simulator of 2D planetary motion. Use WASD to move and SPACE to jump
Originally this was meant to be a full game, but this is all, I think, it will ever become.
As always, feel free to use anything you find.

This Immortal Coil (full game)
A game I made in high school.
It's a hack and slash boss rush. When you die you go back to the beginning.
If the game glitches out, try resizing the window.
